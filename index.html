USS CANNON

About USS CANNON

USS CANNON was named for the Astronomer Annie Jump Cannon (1863-1941), who became known as the "census taker of the sky", was an American astronomer who classified around 350,000 stars manually. She developed the Harvard spectral system, which is used to classify stars today. Cannon inspired many women to work in astronomy, at a time when gender biases greatly favored men in the space industry. USS CANNON was launch January 1st 2020. Earth had entered into a period of peace and prostarity after several years of unrest across the globe. The Earths population started to look more towards the stars and sent several large vessals out into deep space to explore and offer peace offerings to any alien life encountered and report back with daily updates. These are it's logs.



Each day the Captain of the USS CANNON entered a date to log the important events of the day. The ship encountered many different type of alien life and planets along its journey as well as logged new star formations seen in deep space.
